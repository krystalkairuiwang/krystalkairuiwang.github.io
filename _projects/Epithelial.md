Spring 2026 - Present

Interested in tissue engineering and its wide applications in basic research as well as clincal treatments, I joined the <a href="https://gerechtlab.duke.edu/">Gerecht Lab</a> as an undergraduate researcher. We are building <i>in vitro</i> small artery models by seeding epithelial cells into tubular fibrin grafts, which will provide more physiological insights compared to traditional <i>in vitro</i> models. Currently in training, I have helped with cell passaging and differentiating, preparing different media, electrospinning, and working with forceps under the biosafety hoods to cannulate the grafts unto a bioreactor.
